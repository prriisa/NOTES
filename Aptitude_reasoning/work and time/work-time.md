# WORK AND TIME

## WORK

the total amount of work a single person or a group needs to do.

## TIME
total amount of time a group or a person take to do that work.

## EFFICIENCY

the rate at which a person or a group can do that certain task is efficiency.

---
## HOW TO SOLVE

to solve work and time questions we can use 2 concept those are `fraction method` and `lcm method`.

---
## FRACTION METHOD

`ques.` A can do a work in 10 days whereas B can do same work in 20 days, if both of them work together how many days will it taks to complete the work

```
lets says the total work is 1 unit.

a = 10 days
b = 20 days

efficiency = 

10 days = 1 unit
work done by A in 1 day = 1/10

20 days = 1 unit
work done by B in 1 day = 1/20

total work done by both in 1 day = 1/10 + 1/ 20 = 3/20

to complete 1 unit work = 1/(3/20) = 20/3 = 6.6days
```

## LCM METHOD 

`ques.` A can do a work in 10 days whereas B can do same work in 20 days, if both of them work together how many days will it taks to complete the work

```
A = 10 days
B = 20 days

total work = LCM of the value of A and B time taken to do the work = 20 unit

efficiency of A =

10 day = 20 unit
1 day = 2 unit

efficiency of B = 

20 days = 20 unit
1 day  = 1 unit

efficiency of both together = 3 unit...

days taken to complete 20 unit work = 20/3 = 6.6 days
```
