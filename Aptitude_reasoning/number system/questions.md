### 1. if "n" be any natural number, then by which largest number (n^3-n) is divisible by ?
```
a. 3
b. 6
c. 12
d. 18
```
lets put the value of n as natural number and calculate the result.

1^3-1 = 0   (1)
2^3-2 = 6   (2)

from (2) we get that 12, 18 can't be the answer, so from 3, 6 the largest number is 6 that means the answer here is `6`.
